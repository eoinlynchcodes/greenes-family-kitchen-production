Deliciously Fluffy Pancakes

We combine fresh Irish sources ingredients with a strong emphasis on welfare and sustainability. The flour is native Irish and comes from Kilkenny and the eggs come from our own farm in Westmeath. Our hens are free range and give us award-winning quality eggs. So, from our kitchen to yours, enjoy!

  • Suitable for Vegetarians
  • Made with flour from Kilkenny, Ireland.
  • Made with eggs from our own farm in Westmeath - greenesfreerangeeggs.ie
